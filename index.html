When it produces 100 units, a firm's total variable cost is $300 and its total fixed cost is $2700. What is the average total cost?

🧮 MATH REVEAL

Total Cost = TFC + TVC

Total Cost = $2700 + $300 = $3000

Average Total Cost = Total Cost ÷ Quantity

ATC = $3000 ÷ 100 = $30

Correct answer: a) $30
